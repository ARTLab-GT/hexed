Detailed Description

Represents a vertex in a deformed grid.

Used by Deformed_elements to manage data that is shared by nodal neighbors, including position and artificial viscosity coefficient.

Note
Construction and ownership should be managed by the Transferable_ptr member class.

void hexed::Vertex::eat ( Vertex &  other)

Specify that another vertex represents the same grid point as *this.

*this will acquire other's resources:

  • All Transferable_ptrs pointing to other will be changed to point to this.
  • All Non_transferable_ptrs pointing to other will be nullifyd.
  • The mass of other will be added to that of *this.
  • The poss will be averaged, weighted by mass.
